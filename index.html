Features

Everything you need for great PRDs

From initial idea capture to CPO-level review, Specwright covers the entire PRD lifecycle.

💬

Guided Interviews

AI-driven interviews systematically capture requirements, ensuring you don't miss critical details. Dynamic follow-up questions adapt to your responses.

📄

Multiple Templates

Choose from One-Pagers for quick validation, Standard PRDs for full specifications, or Technical Specs for engineering handoff.

🧠

AI-Powered Review

Get gap analysis, clarity scoring, and CPO-level feedback. Identify weak areas before stakeholder review.

👥

Collaboration

Built-in comments, approval workflows, and status management. Track blockers and get sign-off from stakeholders.

📈

Readiness Scoring

Automated readiness assessment tells you when a PRD is ready for development, with specific improvement suggestions.

📦

Export Anywhere

Export to Markdown for integration with your existing documentation tools, wikis, and version control.
