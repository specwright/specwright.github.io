Design Principles from the Literature

Problem-First Framing

Marty Cagan's SVPG methodology: validate the problem before investing in solutions. Our interview structure enforces this sequence.

Specification Completeness

Karl Wiegers' requirements checklists inform our gap analysis. Every PRD is evaluated against proven completeness criteria.

Spec-First Development

With AI coding assistants (Cursor, Claude Code), PRD quality directly impacts code quality. Better specs = better AI output.

Portfolio Coherence

A beautiful interface for serious PRD work

💬

Guided Interviews

AI-driven interviews systematically capture requirements, ensuring you don't miss critical details. Dynamic follow-up questions adapt to your responses.

📄

Multiple Templates

One-Pagers for quick validation, Standard PRDs for full specifications, or Technical Specs for engineering handoff. Each with tailored interview questions.

Get gap analysis, clarity scoring, and CPO-level feedback. Identify weak areas before stakeholder review with specific improvement suggestions.
